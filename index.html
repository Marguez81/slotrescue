How SlotRescue works

SlotRescue keeps your diary fuller by instantly offering cancelled slots to customers who already have future bookings, with almost no extra admin.

Appointment cancelled

A customer cancels their booking and a slot becomes free.

Find ideal candidates

SlotRescue identifies customers with later appointments who could come earlier.

Send an offer

Customers receive a message offering the earlier slot.

Slot filled automatically

The first customer who confirms gets the slot, and your schedule stays full.
