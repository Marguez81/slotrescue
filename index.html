Cancelled appointments cost businesses thousands every year

When a customer cancels at the last minute, the available slot often remains empty. Even when other customers would gladly take an earlier appointment, they simply never discover the newly available slot in time.

A clinic that loses just two £100 appointments per week can lose over £10,000 per year.

How SlotRescue works

1. Appointment cancelled

A booked slot becomes available in your diary.

2. Customers notified

People with future bookings receive a message offering the earlier time.

3. First confirmation wins

The first customer who confirms gets the earlier appointment.

4. Chain reaction

The original slot becomes available and may fill another appointment.

A smart waiting list that works automatically

Many customers would accept an earlier appointment if one becomes available. SlotRescue automatically contacts customers who already have bookings and prefer an earlier time, then confirms the first response.
