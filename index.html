How SlotRescue works

SlotRescue focuses on one specific problem: automatically recovering cancelled appointment slots by offering them to customers who already have bookings in the future.

  1. Add today’s appointments

    Drop in the jobs you’ve already booked so SlotRescue knows exactly when you’re busy and when you’re free.

  2. Build your short‑notice list

    Collect customers who like being called in earlier. We keep that list ready for when slots open.

  3. Let cancellations auto‑fill

    When someone cancels, we message your list and confirm the first reply. Your diary stays full automatically.
